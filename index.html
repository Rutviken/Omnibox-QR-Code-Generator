Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the Chrome extension?

Simply click on the "Get Chrome Extension" button and follow the instructions.

Is it free to use?

Yes, our extension is completely free to use.

How do I customize my QR codes?

Currently, our extension generates standard QR codes, but we plan to introduce customization options in future updates. Share your feedback and suggestions with us!

How do I pin the extension to the toolbar?

After installing the extension, click the puzzle piece icon in the upper right corner of Chrome, find the Omnibox QR Code Generator in the list, and click the pin icon next to it to pin it to the toolbar.
